About the database

This is an experiment of converting epigraphic data from Wikidata to TEI XML. Here I am to include inscriptions that I retrieve from Wikidata and can be searched through the search interface and browse in the Epigraphic Archive as well.
Basically, the workflow I am following would be to retrieve items in a TEI XML format and then to upload them to the Epigraphic Archive. As of now, the latter contains only inscriptions that I have manually and randomly created (fake insctiptions) and therefore is not of any utility yet.
Once I figured out how to access Wikidata (API?) I'll now start the process.
